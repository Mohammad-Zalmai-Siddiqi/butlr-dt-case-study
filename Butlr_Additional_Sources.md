## Key Insights
- Butlr focuses on privacy-first sensing using thermal technology  
- The platform avoids collecting personally identifiable information (PII)  
- AI and machine learning are used to generate actionable insights from occupancy data  
- Organizations use Butlr to reduce energy costs, improve efficiency, and optimize space utilization  
- The solution supports sustainability goals by reducing energy waste  

## Timeline
- Founded as an MIT Media Lab spinout  
- Developed the Heatic™ thermal sensor technology  
- Expanded into commercial smart building and workplace solutions
